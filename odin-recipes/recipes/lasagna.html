Lasagna

Description

It all started when I was 6 years old. My mom died in child birth and my only companion was the golden haired horse she left behind. Then my dad got cancer and it was just me and the horse. He later escaped from the ranch and when we found him again he'd been mauled by bears. During those hard times I was always comforted by the smell of my maid's homemade lasagna. Rich, cheesy, tomatoey, meaty, perfect for a potluck or house party.
